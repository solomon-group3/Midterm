Welcome to Cabuyao!

Your Home in Southern Luzon







Home

Well, what really is Cabuyao City? As a city located in the southern luzon, Cabuyao offers connections to many well known cities and tourist spots. In fact, Cabuyao offers a short travel time to many famous places such as Solenad, Nuvali, Alabang, Mall of Asia, Tagaytay, and many more. All of these can be reached only within one to two hours of travel with light traffic. So? What are you waiting for? Let's explore Cabuyao now!



Gallery

Looking for a place that covers many travel spots? Might you be a student or a millenial looking for adventure or a place that offers access to all your needs, Cabuyao is the place to go! Check pictures that show the magnificant city of Cabuyao, the bridge that connects the south *take note that Cabuyao is not really called "the bridge"*.




Maps

As travellers, passerbys, and even future citizens of Cabuyao, exploring the offered geographical location can surely lead you to great adventures. Despite being a city that directly follows the national highway, Cabuyao has those turns that will lead you to certain barangays and hidden spots that might even become one of your regular spots.
